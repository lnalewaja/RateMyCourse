Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I got a message while adding a course saying I don't have permissions!

To create a new course for rating, please log in using an active account from uncc.edu or charlotte.edu.

Q: The AI Chat is not giving me a course overview of the comments!

To access course reviews based on comments, simply type "reviews: course_id" (e.g., "reviews: ITSC-2181"). Please note that this feature is currently in the beta stage, that is why it's case sensitive.

Q: Who is able to review each course?

Only students with verified UNCC email accounts are eligible to submit new reviews for courses on our platform.

Q: Are there any guidelines or criteria for writing a helpful review?

Please focus on providing specific details about your experience in the class, highlighting both positives and negatives, and avoiding personal attacks or inappropriate language. Your review should be informative and helpful to other students in making informed decisions about their course selections.
